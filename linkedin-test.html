🔗 LinkedIn Company Enrichment

📋 Instructions:

  • Upload an Excel file (.xlsx or .xls) containing company data
  • Your Excel file should have at least one column named: companyName, company, name, or storeName
  • Optional columns: location, address, city (helps find correct LinkedIn page)
  • The system will search for each company's LinkedIn page and extract detailed information
  • Results will include: company description, industry, size, headquarters, website, followers, etc.
